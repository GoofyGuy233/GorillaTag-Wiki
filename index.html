FAQ

How do I join the community?

Join the official Discord at discord.gg/gorillatag — it's the ONLY official channel. Check #announcements for news and read #rules carefully.

What's the Code of Conduct?

No racism, sexism, homophobia, or bigotry. No cheats or mods. Don't harass players or grief lobbies. Be a good Gorilla and have fun — that's all we ask.

How do I report a player?

Use the big board in-game to mute or report players. You can also submit a report outside the game via the reporting form on the Discord server.

The board shows 0 players online — what do I do?

Check your headset's time settings, restart your router, and make sure all k-ID permissions are granted. If issues persist, open a support ticket at support.gorillatagvr.com.
